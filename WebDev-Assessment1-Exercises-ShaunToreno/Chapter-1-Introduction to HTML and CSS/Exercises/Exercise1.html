Low Fat Pancakes

An easy to cook recipe for low fat pancakes that focuses
reducing the fat content without sacrificing the taste and texture.

Ingredients:

Follow these cooking steps:

  1. Gather all the mentioned ingredients.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk the egg, oil, non-fat milk, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients and stir well to thoroughly combine. Don't overmix, as this will develop the gluten in the batter and make the pancakes tough and doughy.
  5. Rest the mixed batter for at least 10 minutes before cooking. This resting time allows the milk to hydrate the flour and lets the raising agents distribute throughout the batter.
  6. Heat an electric non-stick griddle to 375F, or use a large non-stick skillet over medium heat on the stove.
  7. Pour in 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  8. Cook until bubbles form and the edges appear dry.
  9. Flip the pancakes and cook for another 1 to 2 minutes.
  10. Serve warm with your favorite sides and enjoy!.
