Overview

The game follows Lee Everett, a convict that was on his way out of Atlanta with the police until the driver suddenly runs into a zombie, or walker as they are soon referred to in the series, causing them to steer off the highway and crash, leading him to his first experience with the walking dead as the driver rises from the dead and attacks him. Then he meets a young girl named Clementine who he decides to bring along as he didnt want to leave her all alone in such a dangerous world and help her find her parents. Over their journey, together they meet various survivors all who share a common goal for surviving in this new world they suddenly found themselves in.

Key Information

  • Title: The Walking Dead: Season One
  • Developer: Telltale Games
  • Year of Release: 2012
  • Genre: Adventure, Interactive Drama
  • Setting: The game's story takes place in Georgia, USA during a zombie apocalypse.
  • Main Characters: Lee Everett and Clementine Marsh

Starting Characters

  • Lee Everett - The protagonist, a former university professor and convicted criminal.
  • Clementine Marsh - A young girl who is looking for her parents with Lee's help.
  • Kenny - A fisherman who prioritizes his family above all else.
  • Katjaa - Kenny's wife, a Belgian veterinarian that loves her son.
  • Duck - Kenny and Katjaa's energetic son.
